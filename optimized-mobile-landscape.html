What is an Airport Transfer?

Airport transfers are pre-booked methods of transport for picking up passengers from an airport and dropping them off at their holiday resort, hotel, or chosen destination for a set price. You can also book a return transfer, which will take you from your accommodation back to the airport - simply add it on when you book your initial journey, and a driver will be at your booked location in time to whisk you back.

Which Airport Transfer Do I Choose?

Shared Shuttles

  • Our cheapest holiday transfer, great for solo travellers. Shuttles serve resorts and hotels only
  • Makes some stops along the way. Upgrade to a Speedy Shuttle. Wait time of up to 50 minutes
  • Enjoy no hidden fees or extra costs. Pay in advance
  • You will be travelling with a number of other passengers

Private Car

  • Ideal for couples and families. Fast, direct transfer to accommodation
  • No stops except your own. No queues. Upgrade to an Executive or Luxury transfer
  • Paid for in advance (no taxi meters)
  • Seats up to 4 passengers

Private Minibus

  • Suitable for groups and big families. No need for multiple cars
  • No stops and no queues. Direct to your hotel or private accommodation
  • Buy before you fly. Low cost when split between the whole party
  • Seats up to 16 passengers

Wheelchair Adapted

  • Specially adapted van for passengers with restricted mobility
  • Direct door to door service. Assistance on hand
  • All costs and fees paid for in advance, no hidden costs
  • Seats up to 4 passengers

Private Coach

  • Great for wedding parties, special birthdays, school trips and much more
  • Direct to your accommodation with all your travellers
  • Affordable cost per person when at full capacity
  • Seats up to 54 passengers
